What We Provide

A2SV offers free remote and in-person education to help African students develop their digital skills. The program starts with a community education program that gives students the chance to build real-world software projects. This pathway not only teaches students valuable skills, but it also opens up opportunities to interview with top tech companies.

In-Person Education

In-person program for talented students from Addis Ababa University, AASTU, and University of Ghana, offering hands-on training on campus.

Remote Education

Remote education allows students from all over the world access to its high-quality education through a virtual setting.

Community Education

The Community Education Program is the entry to A2SV, allowing students to start their learning journey until the next intake season.
